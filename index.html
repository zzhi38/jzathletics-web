Who We Are

We are a 501 (c3) nonprofit based in Southern California run by two High School Students. We strive to help younger students gain access and exposure to athletics by giving free basketball lessons and fundraising for sports equipment in low income areas.

Lessons

We offer free one on one sessions in basketball fundamentals. They can last between 30 minutes to 1 hour. In these lessons, we will cover important basketball fundamentals while teaching important life skills to help you child succeed in the future. Click the button below to signup, or contact us below!
